About Us

Thengisayangaye Primary School Established in 2000 in the Thokoza area of Ulundi, the school began with just Grade 1 and two teachers under the leadership of Mrs. M Sikhosana, who retired in 2014. Now a public school for boys and girls aged five to twelve (Grade R to 7), the school aims to lay a strong and effective foundation for holistic child development. Its mission is to help learners reach their full potential by promoting responsibility, respect, discipline, and confidence. The school also strives for excellence and efficiency through meaningful educator-learner interaction, support for socialisation, identity formation, community engagement, and preparing learners to thrive in society as responsible adults.

  • Founded in 2000
  • Over 700 students enrolled
  • Highly qualified, caring staff
